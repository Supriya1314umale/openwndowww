HEALING STARTS HERE

Professional Qualified Therapist That You Trust

Every clinician at The Open Window goes through a rigorous hiring process that assesses not only clinical competence, but ethical grounding, reflective capacity, and alignment with our values. We look for therapists who can hold complexity, work with humility, and prioritise client wellbeing over performance.
Once onboarded, clinicians are supported through regular supervision, reflective practice, and clear ethical guidelines. We prioritise therapeutic depth, cultural sensitivity, and sustainable care, because trust is built not just in the room, but behind the scenes too.

MATCH WITH YOUR THERAPIST

How It Works

At The Open Window, care is intentional from the first interaction to the last session. Our process is designed to feel clear,thoughtful, and steady,not rushed or transactional.

01

Reach Out

You begin by sharing what brings you here. This can be brief. You do not need to have the right words or a fully formed goal.

We simply want to understand where you are starting from. You can reach out to us on WhatsApp or on our website.

Thoughtful Matching

We carefully match you with a therapist based on your concerns, preferences, lived context, and therapeutic needs. Matching is not automated alone. It is guided by clinical judgement and care.

02

First Session

The initial session focus on understanding your history, current challenges, pace, and readiness.

There is no pressure to disclose everything at once. Therapy begins where you are.

Ongoing Work

Your therapist works with you using evidence-based approaches tailored to your needs. Together, you clarify goals, explore patterns, and build emotional steadliness at a pace that feels sustainable.

03

Review and Adjust

Therapy is collaborative. You and your therapist regularly reflect on progress,adjust direction when needed, and decide together what next steps look like.

Closing with Care

When the time feels right,therapy moves towards closure intentionally.Endings are processed with care, ensuring integration rather than abrupt withdrawal. The timeline looks different for everyone. For some, Therapy is short and focused. For others, it unfolds over a longer period.The pace and duration are shaped collaboratively, based on your needs,goals and readiness.
